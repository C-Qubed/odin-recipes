Pad Thai

Description

Created in the 1930's in Thailand by Plaek Philbunsongkhram, Pad Thai is a noodle dish loved around world.

There are many variations on the original recipe, but in its most basic form, Pad Thai is rice noodles, various vegetables, and a source of protein.

The recipe listed here is a twist on the original recipe that is low FODMAP to accomodate people with IBS.
